# 🎵 GlowEdge

**Music-reactive edge lighting visualizer for Android. Free & open source.**

GlowEdge displays glowing, animated light effects around your screen edges while music plays — on top of any app.

## ✨ Features
- Live music-reactive glow around screen edges
- Works as an overlay on top of any music app
- 4 built-in color profiles: Neon, Sunset, Ocean, Royal Gold
- Clean onboarding with clear permission explanations
- Lightweight — no ads, no tracking, nothing recorded or stored

## 📥 Download
Go to the **Actions** tab → open the latest successful build → download the **GlowEdge-APK** artifact.

## 🔒 Permissions
| Permission | Why |
|---|---|
| Display over other apps | To draw the glow on top of your apps |
| Audio access | To react to the music (visualization only — never recorded) |
| Notifications | Keeps the service alive with a quick Stop button |
